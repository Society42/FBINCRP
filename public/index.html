NORTHCAROLINA ROLEPLAY
SERVER INFO
North Carolina State Roleplay (NCRP) is a thriving FiveM community founded by three close friends, each with over five years of development experience. This dynamic server delivers a rich, immersive roleplaying experience inspired by the diverse culture and scenic beauty of North Carolina. With meticulously crafted custom scripts, detailed maps, and a dedicated team ensuring smooth gameplay, NCRP fosters a welcoming environment for roleplayers of all skill levels. Whether you're patrolling as law enforcement, building a business, or exploring creative storylines, NCRP stands out as a premier destination for FiveM enthusiasts.
SERVER HISTORY
North Carolina State Roleplay (NCRP) was founded by three friends with over five years of development experience, united by a vision to create a top-tier FiveM community. Starting with custom scripts and a focus on immersive gameplay, the server quickly grew into a hub for quality roleplay inspired by the charm of North Carolina. Through dedication to innovation and community feedback, NCRP has evolved into a vibrant space for players to connect, create, and explore endless storylines.
