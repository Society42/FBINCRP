About the FBI

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) is the United States' premier law enforcement agency tasked with national security, criminal investigation, and counterterrorism efforts.

Our Divisions

Hostage Rescue Team (HRT)

The HRT specializes in high-risk situations, including hostage rescues and terrorist threats.

Cyber Crime Division (CCD)

Focused on combating cyber threats, including hacking, online fraud, and cyberterrorism.

K9 Unit

Trained dogs assist in narcotic detection, bomb squads, and search-and-rescue missions.

Recruitment Office

FBI actively recruits dedicated personnel for positions across the agency's vast network.

Internal Affairs (IA)

Investigating misconduct within the Bureau to maintain the highest ethical standards.
